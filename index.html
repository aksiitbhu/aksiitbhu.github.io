About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am Dr. A. K. Singh, an accomplished educator and researcher in computer science. Currently, I serve as an Associate Professor at Graphic Era Deemed to be University, bringing over a decade of teaching experience to the table.

I earned my Ph.D. from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, where my research focused on Network Science particularly dynamic social networks. My academic journey continued with a postdoc position at King's College London, United Kingdom.

My work has been published in leading conferences (AAAI-ICWSM24, ICSA-24) and journals (Neurocomputing, Multimedia Tools and Applications), reflecting my expertise in Machine Learning, NLP, Network Science, and Social Networks Analysis. I am passionate about staying at the forefront of my field, regularly attending international workshops, conferences, and FDPs. My goal is to inspire my students, igniting their curiosity and enthusiasm for Computer Science Research.
