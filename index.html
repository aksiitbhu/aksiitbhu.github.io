Dr. Ashwini Kumar Singh

Associate Professor, Department of Computer Science
Sitare University, India

I am a researcher in Computational Social Science, Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, and Network Science. I completed my Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi, and held a Postdoctoral Research position at King’s College London (2022–2024). My work focuses on dynamic social networks, influence maximization, toxicity detection, and generative AI systems.
